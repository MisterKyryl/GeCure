Education

Our team comprises experienced pedagogues who have spent years in the practical teaching of undergraduate, graduate, and PhD students in the fields of microbial genetics, biotechnology, cell biology, and biochemistry. We recognize that the availability of skilled personnel is crucial for any research institution. Hence, we aim to contribute to the development of the next generations of Ukrainian molecular biologists by making our facilities available to students from Lviv universities specializing in life sciences. This opportunity allows them to prepare their theses and gain cutting-edge experience.
